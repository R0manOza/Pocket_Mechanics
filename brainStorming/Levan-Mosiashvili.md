## H1: Hassle

*What process in your daily life is tedious, error-prone, or needlessly manual?*

**WHO has this problem?**  
University students in Kutaisi and Tbilisi who work part time jobs or freelance and need to track income, expenses, and deadlines across multiple platforms like bank apps, cash, and online payments.

**PROBLEM in one sentence:**  
Students juggle scattered financial information across apps and cash which leads to missed payments, poor budgeting, and no clear understanding of their monthly spending.

**CURRENT SOLUTION:**  
They use notes apps, spreadsheets, screenshots of transactions, or nothing at all. Some rely only on bank apps which do not include cash spending. Budgeting is inconsistent and usually abandoned after a short time.

**AI ANGLE:**  
Multimodal input where users upload screenshots, photos of receipts, or voice notes such as "I spent 20 GEL on transport" and the system structures everything automatically into categories. The system must include confidence scoring so that uncertain classifications are confirmed by the user instead of guessed.

**WHY IT MATTERS:**  
Better financial awareness early in life leads to stronger long term habits. Even saving a small amount monthly is meaningful for students. Reducing manual effort increases the chance that users stay consistent.

## H2: Hardship

*What problem do you see in your community or country that affects real people?*

**WHO has this problem?**  
Drivers in smaller Georgian cities like Zestaponi and Samtredia who rely on informal car repair shops without standardized diagnostics.

**PROBLEM in one sentence:**  
Car owners cannot clearly describe vehicle issues which leads to misdiagnosis, unnecessary repairs, and extra costs.

**CURRENT SOLUTION:**  
Drivers explain problems verbally and mechanics rely on experience and guesswork. Repairs are often done through trial and error and sometimes require multiple visits.

**AI ANGLE:**  
Audio based analysis where users record the sound of their car such as engine noise or braking and the system compares it with known patterns of mechanical issues. Results must be presented as possible causes with confidence levels instead of definitive answers.

**WHY IT MATTERS:**  
Car repairs are expensive relative to income and avoiding even one unnecessary repair can save a significant amount of money. It also reduces dependence on inconsistent service quality.

## H3: Horizon

*What new capability does multimodal AI unlock that was impossible 2 years ago?*

**WHO has this problem?**  
Local tourists and hikers exploring regions like Imereti and Racha who encounter landmarks or locations without any explanation or signage.

**PROBLEM in one sentence:**  
People visit interesting places but cannot understand what they are seeing which causes them to miss cultural and historical context.

**CURRENT SOLUTION:**  
They try to search online if they know what to search for, ask locals, or ignore the location entirely. Many places are not documented online.

**AI ANGLE:**  
Vision based recognition combined with retrieval where a user points their camera at a location and the system identifies it or finds similar matches and provides context such as history and significance. The system must handle low confidence cases carefully because many locations are not well documented.

**WHY IT MATTERS:**  
This turns any location into an interactive learning experience and encourages exploration of lesser known areas while preserving cultural awareness.
