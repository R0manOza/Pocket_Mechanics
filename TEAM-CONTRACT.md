## Accountability and Consequences

**If a team member misses a deadline:**
They post an update within 12 hours with a new ETA and what help is needed. The team may re-scope or reassign tasks to protect the submission.

**If a team member is unreachable for more than 3 days without explanation:**
The team reassigns their tasks, documents the change in the repo, and flags the issue in the next instructor/TA check-in if it impacts delivery.

**If a team member consistently under-delivers:**
Work is broken into smaller tasks with explicit acceptance criteria; if no improvement after 1 week, responsibilities are rebalanced and recorded in the repo history.
